# Investrade Future Improvements & Roadmap

This document outlines suggested features and enhancements for the Investrade Admin Dashboard and Platform.

## 1. Interactive Visualizations (Charts)
- **Revenue Growth**: Line chart for monthly revenue trends.
- **User Demographics**: Pie chart showing Founder vs. Investor split.
- **Startup Distribution**: Bar chart for startup stages (Pre-seed, Seed, etc.).

## 2. Actionable Management
- **Startup Verification**: Add "Verify" button to grant startups a trusted badge.
- **Payment Approval**: One-click approval for manual PayPal payments that triggers access codes and automated emails.
- **User Moderation**: Ability to suspend or ban users directly from the dashboard.

## 3. Advanced Reporting
- **Data Export**: "Export to CSV/Excel" buttons for financial and enrollment tables.
- **Profitability Tracking**: Net revenue after platform fees or expenses.

## 4. AI & Match-Making
- **Investor Matcher**: AI-driven suggestions matching startups to investors based on focus and ticket size.
- **Pitch Deck Summarizer**: Automated summaries for startup data rooms.

## 5. CRM & Communication
- **Direct Emailing**: Send emails to users directly from their table row.
- **Newsletter Sync**: Sync training registrations with Mailchimp/Resend.
